# Global Bank - Corporate Transfer Policy 2024

## 1. Introduction
This policy outlines the procedures for executing corporate transfers within Global Bank. All employees must adhere to these guidelines to ensure security and compliance with international banking regulations.

## 2. Standard Operating Procedures
1. All transfers above $10,000 must be authorized by a branch manager.
